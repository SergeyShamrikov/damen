Damen Business course

The Damen Business Course is a unique opportunity to get to know Damen Shipyards Group better and boost your own professional development, even before you begin your career.

The Damen Business Course is primarily intended for technology students who have just begun their last year of course work for their masters degree. But you may also be eligible to participate even if your affinity for technology, vessels, and water has not extended to your formal course work. We are seeking enterprising students with an innovative outlook, team players who do not shy away from adventure.

Naturally, academic excellence and social engagement are also important to us.

Using an extensive selection process, we select the 15 most suitable candidates from all of the applicants.

The successful candidates are then sent on a rollercoaster ride that rockets them from one Damen branch or activity to another. During this intensive programme, Damen’s senior management give the participants assignments involving current issues facing the organisation. At the end of the programme, the participants present their advice or results to an Executive Panel in teams. The work on the projects, as well as the feedback received on their work and the results of the various tests that will be used all add up to an experience that most of the participants will not soon forget.

Discover yourself and the opportunities Damen has to offer: see whether the Damen Business Course is something for you.
